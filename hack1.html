SAACHACK (Security/Smart city Augmentation reality Artificial intelligence Cloud HACKathon) 2018 is a 24 hours Hackathon organized by Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology, Chennai. The motto of SAACHACK is Making Ideas into Reality.

About RIT
Education today is an indispensable springboard to advancement. We, at RAJALAKSHMI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, have dedicated ourselves to impart technical and engineering education at its best. We strive to provide the students with the right environment to acquire knowledge, the spirit of excellence and impart quality technical education that fosters learning and research. Overview:
The major objective of the SAACHACK includes exploring new technologies, driving business innovation, bringing about civic involvement, fostering awareness, and encouraging ownership for a social cause. The Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology will provide a platform to showcase the coding and implementation skills and provided excellent way to motivate student community and making learning interesting. SAACHACK helps in exploring new ideas and finding new talents. The major benefit for RIT is to enrich practical ability of the student of various levels.
