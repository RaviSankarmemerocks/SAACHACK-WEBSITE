F. Augmented Reality

F01. A mobile application for textbooks that enhances the reading experience for school students.

Description: By scanning their physical textbooks, students can highlight, share, add notes, and watch videos, creating a more immersive learning experience. Steps to do • Create an Android App • Scan the text book • Extract words from the book using OCR • Connect to internet and search wiki based on the keyword.
